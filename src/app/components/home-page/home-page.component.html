What is your Idea ?

What can classify as an Idea ?

  • Solves pain points in existing process/ implementation.

  • Can be anything out of the box/ unrelated to regular work but somehow relevant to airline business domain.

  • Can be a totally new technology trend.

  • Can be an existing idea but totally new to KLM or airline business domain.

Presentation & Shortlist

  • A bi weekly connect to provide a pitching platform.

  • Shortlisting based on uniqueness, potential and feasibility.

  • If there is no challenge in implementation of an idea it can be pursued by the individual

  • Shortlisted Ideas which need support will be facilitated resources to be developed further into working prototype

  • Ideas can be refined and considered again in case they become eligible based on shortlist criteria.

Development & Internal Demo

  • Small team will be assigned to develop idea into working prototype.

  • Scope and Timeline should be agreed upon by development team and management.

  • Once team has the prototype ready, there will be an internal demo and review will be done.

  • When a working prototype has been refined and finalised it can be presented to other stakeholders (Eg. Other business domains within TCS KLM account, Other TCS accounts).
